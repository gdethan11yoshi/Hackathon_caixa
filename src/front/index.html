To find the respective classes inside the campus, first you need to take into account the building number, which will follow a clockwise order beginning from building 1.

Next is the floor where the class is located, which will usually be accessible from both staircases and elevators.

Finally, each wing is labelled on the multiple maps scattered inside each building (e.g. A, B, ... F, etc.), with the ordered classes on it.

What is done in each building?

Each building has its own purpose and its own usual places:

  • chevron_right

    Agustín de Bentacourt (1): Here lies the electronics, mechanics and robotics laboratories, as well as several classrooms and departments.

  • chevron_right

    Sabatini (2): Most associations and laboratories are found here.

  • chevron_right

    Rey Pastor (3): Here is the library, the Vice-Rectorate of Studies and also the Main Services of Rectorate.

  • chevron_right

    Torres Quevedo (4): The Computing Department and many more engineering labs are here.

  • chevron_right

    Padre Soler (5): Here is the Auditorium and the Grade room, where most speeches and events are held.

  • chevron_right

    Sports Center (6): This is the sports facility, you can find the gym, the pool and more here.

  • chevron_right

    Juan Benet (7): Lectures and computer labs are held here.
